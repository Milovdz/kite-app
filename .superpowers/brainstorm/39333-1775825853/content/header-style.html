A — Pill badge next to wind speed

A rounded pill badge ("✓ Onshore" / "✗ Offshore") sits to the right of the wind/gust numbers. The direction arrow also changes colour — teal for safe, red for offshore. Clean and compact.

B — Inline with compass label

The safety label is appended directly to the compass text under the arrow: "SW · Onshore ✓" or "SE · Offshore ⚠". The arrow colour also changes. No extra element — minimal but coherent with the tile style.
